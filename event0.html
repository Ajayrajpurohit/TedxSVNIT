Stop & Think

Theme of our TEDx event is ‘Stop and Think’. In a fast paced life, many interesting things pass by us and we go on without noticing them. But there are some things which require our attention. ‘Oh! This could also happen’, ‘Oh! How could one manage to do so’, ‘Oh! This is fascinating, why haven’t I noticed earlier’ are some of the reactions which naturally come after noticing these things. For example, while browsing Facebook timeline, we often stumble upon some article, which amazes us and we took sometime to ponder over the things which we saw. ’Stop and Think’ means to stop and think about the things which we normally fails to notice in our daily lives.
