Speakers



Dr. Rachna Arora

Dr. Rachna Arora has been working tirelessly for GIZ - a German organization aimed at solving complex international problems for sustainable development - for the past fifteen years. This has allowed Dr. Arora to follow her passion of helping people in need as her work has become instrumental in shaping the future in a variety of fields including energy, economic development and world peace. Having met ex-Indian president Pranab Mukherjee, Dr. Arora is a renowned individual around the country- this year, at TEDxSVNIT she will talk about her work and how we can all contribute to society in our own little way.


Awards and Recognition

  • In 2014, Arora and her team at GIZ-IGEP had the chance to meet then-President Pranab Mukherjee at the Rashtrapati Bhawan to discuss various issues India is facing and ways they can be resolved. This involved discussion on topics such as effective e-waste management and damage control.
  • Dr. Arora helped organize the Circular Economy Mission (CEM) 2018 in India. This is the first time such an event has happened in our nation, giving a bold statement to the world on how India is adapting in times of change.
  • In the 2017 NITI Aayog strategy paper on resource efficiency, Dr. Arora’s name is mentioned on the first page itself where the principal advisor, Ratan P. Watsal has said that Dr. Arora is one of the two people that may be consulted on anything mentioned in the paper.

Preveen Pandita

Preveen Pandita is a leading mentalist who has performed incredible feats on the biggest of stages in India including India’s Got Talent, Bigg Boss and AXN’s Ripley’s Believe it or not. One of the most famous mentalists in Asia, Pandita is especially known for his TV special “Mystical Stranger”.
Pandita combines clever effects with hilarious and elite class humour and is really active on National TV Shows like Trick Or Real (Discovery Channel), India’s Got Talent and Bigg Boss. He has also been the brand ambassador for popular companies and for elite products including the E-class Mercedes car.


Awards and Recognition

  • He is the only Indian Mentalist to have won the Ripley’s Believe It or Not Talent Hunt, which he won in 2012.
  • He has been voted as the best illusionist of India and most booked illusionist in Asia by Mid-Day India.
  • He has been invited to many prestigious places like IIT Delhi, IIT Guwahati, IIT Madras, and to many niche groups like the Indian Army, SAARC Summit, IIM’s and has given multiple TEDx talks.

Major Mohommed Ali Shah

A TEDx speaker, A Defence expert and A Defence analyst on many popular Indian News Channels, a well known Theatre and film personality too, an alumni of prestigious IIM Calcutta and a visiting faculty at Subhash Ghai's "Whistling Woods International". He joined the Short Service Commission in the Army to train as a "Commando" and was posted at the Indo-Pak border at the line of control as a young Lieutenant.


Awards and Recognition

  • He was awarded a commendation card for his exceptional and meritorious service to the nation for being unmindful of his own personal safety and going beyond the call of duty while serving with the “Assam rifles” in the North East.
  • He has acted in "Agent Vinod" and in Vishal Bhardwaj's "Haider" which won 5 National awards apart from from several other prestigious film awards. He has recently completed shooting for National School of Drama alumnus and National award winning director Tigmanshu Dhulia's latest film "Yaara" and Jahnu Baruah's " Broken window" produced by Priyanka Chopra.
  • Major Ali won the coveted award of “Best actor” at the prestigious Delhi International Film Festival, “Best actor” at the International Film Festival of Prayag and “Best actor” at the Aligarh Film Saaz International Film Festival.
  • The best thing about Major Shah is that all the talks given by him have received standing ovations from the audience.

Shri Ghanshyam Soni

Ghanshyam Soni is IRS officer currently serving as Deputy Commissioner in Customs Department, Surat . He is very active in multiple fields. Not only has he brought revolutionary improvements in customs and revenues of Surat but also he has been a very active and dedicated social worker. He has been through tough circumstances in early stages of life but it never stopped him from shining and reaching where he is meant to be. He has completed Honors’ and M.A. in history with distinction and thereafter served as a Lecturer for a brief period before becoming an IRS Officer in 2010.


Awards and Recognition

  • Ghanshyam Soni has adopted villages and government schools like KV Ichchhanath to look after their development.
  • For his exceptional work in providing amenities to his village Melawas , he has been honored with Gujarat Ratna Award b World Record Foundation and Awareness Public Charitable Trust in VNSGU.
  • He has also been honored with Global Dignitary Award. He qualified UPSC in 2010 and has been serving an IRS Officer since then. He is actively involved in social activities for environment and differentially abled children.

Pratyush Pillai

Pratyush Pillai is a storyteller who has devoted his entire life to understand why we do what we do and how this can be used to bring positive changes in our day-to-day lives. Experienced in the field of Design Thinking, Pillai currently works as a Design Strategist for Tinkerlabs where he is one of their key leaders. Having studied marine engineering at university, Pillai has worked at several places advertising, branding and design- his client list includes Gillette, Amazon, and India TV.


Awards and Recognition

  • Pillai has an excellent track record at his previous companies and this can be verified by his client list which includes not only Idea Cellular and Mother Dairy but also Asian Paints and Standard Chartered Bank. Pillai’s record of clients speak for themselves on his ability to efficiently do his work.
  • As a recommendation claims, Pillai ‘...leads with his heart.’ This statement, given by the Co-founder and CEO of a firm he has worked with is an indication of the impact Pillai has on the clients he works with, and also shows how easily he connects with those of whom he works with.

Jawwad Patel

Jawwad Patel is one of the finest young innovators of the country. His invention of the self-filling water apparatus caught many eyeballs and gained extensive media coverage – yet, this is only one of his several achievements. Having been honored the National Youth Excellence Award by the Government of India, Patel is determined to use his skills for the betterment of the nation.


Awards and Recognition

  • Government of India Honored with National Youth Award Excellence in Research & Innovation – The Highest Civilian Award for Youth in India.
  • Recipient of Prestigious The Diana Award 2019 in Research and Innovation from British Government, Young Innovator Award 2018 Exemplary Work in Research and Innovation, WIF, Dallas, Texas, United States of America.
  • Inventor of Dewdrop, Asia’s first 3D Printed Smart Atmospheric Water Generator– Recognized and Appreciated globally.
