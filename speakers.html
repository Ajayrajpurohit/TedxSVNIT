Having earned a bachelor’s degree in commerce, Charanjeett was expected to join his father’s business- but he had completely different ambitions. He came to Mumbai- like millions of other Indians- for a career in acting and through a sheer hard work, he has been able to cement his place in the bollywood industry.

Anjan Prakash

Anjan Prakash started her career in Ogilvy Advertising and then co-founded Brown Skins – a film production house in Mumbai which produced several commercials for national and international clients. After 16 years in advertising, she realized her passion was actually in working at the intersection between business, design and nature. Currently she divides her time between being a Masters cum Professional Certification student and a Biomimicry Facilitator. Her talks and workshops are aimed at educating and informing audiences about how to design better products, processes and systems by looking to nature for time-tested solutions. As a facilitator, she holds the intention of bringing together multidisciplinary professionals to design biomimetic products.

Nikhil Malhotra

Renowned for being an amazing father, Nikhil Malhotra was instrumental in the innovation of the healthcare robot Saarthi. His inputs, along with his little son’s, helped the healthcare robot become a reality which has the potential to help millions, maybe even billions of people all over the world. The initial idea of Saarthi was achieved by Malhotra through the numerous questions asked by his four-year old son, Angad, about what a robot could possibly do in the future. Nikhil Malhotra is the creator of Maker’s Lab, a unique Thin-q-bator space within TechMahindra with over 17 years of experience in a variety of technology domains.

Surmai Kaushik

A backpacker, a conservationist, an educator and a marathon runner. While working at Mahindra Susten- Mahindra’s clean technology arm- Surmai Kaushik got the greatest opportunity of her life- to go to Antarctica with Robert Swann. While this may not be everyone’s cup of coffee, it certainly was for Surmai who has been trying to raise awareness of climate change for her entire life. With her background in climate science, she was a perfect pick for the program Mission 2041- which aims to clean up more than 300 million tonnes of Carbon Dioxide. Kaushik consistently saw the horrible effects of global warming on the poor continent of Antarctica which was covered with melting glaciers and destroyed wildlife for native animals. Kaushik has actively pledged to address this problem by bringing up more clean technology in India with the help of Mahindra Susten.

Dhiraj Jalan

Passionate about safety of health and environment, Dhiraj Jalan, the young entrepreneur, has taken multiple initiatives to help address the issues of adulteration and toxicity. From healthy fashion jewellery (Lustreus Ornaments Pvt. Ltd.) to healthy foods (YogaMilk), his journey has been diverse and has only just begun. He believes in building responsible organisations that are conscious of their impact on both, society and surroundings. The 22-year-old speaker is currently working on redefining how health is delivered at your doorsteps without sacrificing convenience. Today, the healthfulness of our food has largely been compromised in the name of convenient consumption and he is striving to set this right.
